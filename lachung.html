Explore Lachung

Lachung is a picturesque mountain village in North Sikkim, located at the confluence of the Lachen and Lachung rivers. Known for its snow-capped peaks, waterfalls, and apple orchards, it's a serene destination and gateway to Yumthang Valley and Zero Point.
